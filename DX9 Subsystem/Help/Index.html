DX9 subsystem
v0.5 for PureBASIC 06.06.07

The DX9 subsystem was written to port games and other applications
written in PureBASIC easily to DX9.It's written completely in PureBASIC.
At the moment the DX9 subsystem supports the complete Sprite-,Sprite3D-,Keyboard-,Mouse-,Joystick- and Sound Library.
The DX9 subsystem is optimized for speed, not for compactness.
